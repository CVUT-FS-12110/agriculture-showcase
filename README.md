## Projects

Projects are listed from newest to oldest.

- **Automated Early Warning System in Fruit Growing Based on Artificial Intelligence and Deep Neural Networks**
  - **CEP code:** QL26010200
  - **Start date:** 2026-01-01
  - **End date:** 2030-12-31
  - **Summary:** The project aims to expand the use of advanced computer vision and data analytics in precision agriculture. It focuses on developing a local modular system for accurate monitoring of harmful organisms in orchards, with an emphasis on planning plant protection product applications. The industrial partner, Agroscan, already operates an information system that recommends plant protection measures based on pest trap data, leaf damage, and meteorological data. The project will extend this system with a new generation of traps equipped with advanced computer vision for accurate automatic real-time pest detection in fruit plantations. Another objective is to evaluate leaf damage using imagery from mobile monitoring platforms.

- **Research and Development of Robotic Pruning and a Precision Agrotechnology System for a New Concept of Fully Fruiting Orchards**
  - **CEP code:** QL26010022
  - **Start date:** 2026-01-01
  - **End date:** 2030-12-31
  - **Summary:** The project develops mechanization, robotization, and automation in apple growing as part of a unique new concept for production orchards using columnar varieties and multi-axis tree forms. It includes a machine for robotic pruning, a solution for gentle removal of detached fruit from the harvesting head, agrotechnology for a new robotic orchard concept in full fruiting, a methodology for regulating fruit set, a proposal for handling and storing mechanically harvested market fruit, completion of a digital twin of a fully fruiting orchard, creation of a 3D tree model for pruning planning and tree development monitoring, and an application for a new columnar apple variety. The project follows QK21010170.

- **Implementation of Field Autonomy Elements into Fruit Production**
  - **CEP code:** QL24010368
  - **Start date:** 2024-01-01
  - **End date:** 2028-12-31
  - **Summary:** The project supports the expected deployment of semi-autonomous and autonomous systems in nurseries and orchards. It focuses on adapting operations, growing areas, and support systems for robotic deployment. By thoroughly preparing operations for these changes, the project aims to enable efficient, long-term, and economically sustainable use of robots. It also responds to societal demand for greener agriculture through locally targeted applications, innovative agrotechnical interventions, reduced traffic intensity, lower fuel consumption, and less manual work.

- **New Orchard Concept Using Technology 4.0**
  - **CEP code:** QK21010170
  - **Start date:** 2021-01-01
  - **End date:** 2025-12-31
  - **Summary:** The project presents a new orchard concept focused on producing high-quality market apples through practical use of sensor technology, targeted interventions, advanced automation, artificial intelligence, and robotics. It addresses the topic comprehensively, from selecting suitable genotypes and varieties of columnar apple trees to designing a new tree support system with future robotic deployment in mind. The goal is to demonstrate fruit-growing approaches with higher efficiency and minimal need for human labor. By developing autonomous systems and orchard monitoring technologies, the project significantly increases the efficiency of interventions at the level of individual trees.

Projects

The portfolio spans pest detection, robotic pruning, autonomous field operation, and complete orchard concepts built around technology 4.0.

Automated Early Warning System in Fruit Growing Based on Artificial Intelligence and Deep Neural Networks

CEP QL26010200 2026-01-01 to 2030-12-31

A local modular system for accurate monitoring of harmful organisms in orchards. The project extends plant-protection recommendation systems with new traps using advanced computer vision for automatic real-time pest detection and evaluates leaf damage from mobile monitoring imagery.

Research and Development of Robotic Pruning and a Precision Agrotechnology System for a New Concept of Fully Fruiting Orchards

CEP QL26010022 2026-01-01 to 2030-12-31

Development of mechanization, robotization, and automation for apple growing using columnar varieties and multi-axis tree forms. Work includes robotic pruning, fruit handling, agrotechnology, a digital twin, 3D tree models, and support for a new columnar apple variety.

Implementation of Field Autonomy Elements into Fruit Production

CEP QL24010368 2024-01-01 to 2028-12-31

Preparation of nurseries and orchards for semi-autonomous and autonomous systems. The project adapts operations, growing areas, and support systems to enable efficient long-term robotic deployment with lower fuel use, reduced manual work, and more targeted applications.

New Orchard Concept Using Technology 4.0

CEP QK21010170 2021-01-01 to 2025-12-31

A comprehensive orchard concept for high-quality market apples using sensor technology, targeted interventions, automation, artificial intelligence, and robotics. The project covers varieties, support systems, monitoring, and intervention efficiency at the level of individual trees.

Methods

From field sensing to actionable models

The projects combine orchard imagery, depth estimation, 3D reconstruction, neural networks, and practical robotic requirements. The result is research that can guide pruning, monitoring, plant protection, and autonomous field operation.
